FAQ

To register for the "Start-Up Surge" event, follow these steps:
A) Visit our official website at Start-Up Surge.
B) Navigate to the registration page and click on the "Register Now" button.
C) Fill out the necessary information in the registration form, including your name, contact details, and a brief overview of your startup idea.
D) Complete the payment process to finalize your registration. The registration fee can be paid securely through our online payment system.
E) Once the payment is successful, you will receive a confirmation email at the provided email address, indicating that your registration is complete.
F) Ensure that you keep the registration confirmation email for your records.

Please note that the registration fee is non-refundable, as outlined in the event rules. If you encounter any issues during the registration process, feel free to contact our support team at supportstartupsurge@isquareit.edu.in for assistance. We look forward to your participation in "Start-Up Surge"!

Additionally, participation in "Start-Up Surge" offers valuable networking opportunities, allowing you to connect with other entrepreneurs, mentors, and potential collaborators.
